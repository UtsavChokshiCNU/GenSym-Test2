Sun C "cc: illegal suffix of output filename"

SYMPTOMS

With Coverage Build enabled, the Sun Forte 6 C compiler stops with the error: 

cc: illegal suffix of output filename

CAUSE

The C compiler has a bug that results in this symptom whenever -compat=5 is used together with -E. BullseyeCoverage runs the compiler with -E.

RESOLUTION

Remove -compat=5 from your compile options.
